Statistically downscaled climate projections from the 
CMIP6 experiment using the ERA5-Land reanalysis

<><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><>

Reference: Loukos et al. (2022)
https://doi.org/10.1016/j.dib.2022.108669

<><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><>

2 socio-economic scenarii:
- ssp245: middle of the road
- ssp585: conventional development (no mitigation)

5 models:
- GFDL: Geophysical Fluid Dynamics Laboratory of NOAA (US)
- IPSL: Institut Pierre Simon Laplace (FR)
- MPI: Max Planck Institute (DE)
- MRI: Meteorological Research Institute (JP)
- UKESM: UK Earth System Modelling project (UK)

2 variables:
- tasmin: Daily Minimum Near-Surface Air Temperature
- tasmax: Daily Maximum Near-Surface Air Temperature
